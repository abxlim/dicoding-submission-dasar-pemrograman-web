Integrated Litter Box System with Disinfectant and Automated Food and Water Dispenser

The Integrated Litter Box is designed to streamline the maintenance of cleanliness and basic needs for pet cats through automatic cleaning, disinfectant spraying, and scheduled food and water dispensing. This device has a PIR sensor to detect the catâ€™s presence, a LiDAR sensor for litter height measurement, a load cell to gauge food and water weight, and an ultrasonic mist maker for disinfection. Testing results indicate a detection accuracy with a 5% error rate, average food and water filling times of 46 and 70 seconds, and a litter filling error rate of 1.89%. These features offer convenience for cat owners by ensuring litter box hygiene and fulfilling essential needs according to a user-set schedule via RTC.

Mini Tank for Landmine Detection Equipped with Dual Communication Tools: Wi-Fi Local Network and 2.4 GHz ZigBee Wireless Antenna

The mini tank for landmine detection is a vehicle used to detect the presence of landmines in a certain area. This mini tank is developed for use in landmine sterilization situations in a given region. The use of mini tanks for landmine detection is crucial in reducing the risk of fatalities caused by landmines, especially in residential areas that are the aftermath of war or conflict. This mini tank is used to search for and locate landmines buried beneath the ground. For safety reasons, the mini tank is operated remotely via a remote control.
